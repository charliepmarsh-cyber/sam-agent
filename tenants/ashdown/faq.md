# Ashdown Electrical Services — Customer FAQs

Sam uses this file when drafting customer-facing text (invoice cover notes, payment reminders, reply drafts). Tone: friendly, plain English, no jargon.

**Q: What are your payment terms?**
Domestic work is payable on completion. Commercial and contract customers are on 30-day terms from invoice date. Details are on every invoice.

**Q: How can I pay?**
Bank transfer (details on the invoice), or card via the payment link on the invoice. We don't take cheques or cash for invoiced work.

**Q: I've been invoiced the wrong amount.**
Reply to the invoice email or ring the office on 01793 000000 and we'll check it against the signed job sheet the same day. *(Sam: any disputed invoice → freeze reminders on that invoice and escalate to Dev.)*

**Q: Can I get a copy invoice or a statement?**
Yes — reply to any of our emails and we'll send one over. *(Sam: copy invoices and statements are autonomous-permitted read-only actions.)*

**Q: Do you charge a call-out fee?**
Emergency call-outs are £85 + VAT for the first hour, then half-hourly. Scheduled work has no call-out fee.

**Q: Are you insured and certified?**
Yes — NICEIC approved contractor, £5m public liability. Certificates available on request. *(Sam: certificate requests → forward to Dev, do not attach documents autonomously.)*

**Q: When will I get my certificate after an installation?**
Electrical installation certificates are issued within 5 working days of final payment. *(Sam: this is why payment matching matters — certificate issuance is blocked on reconciled payment.)*

**Q: I've paid but you've sent a reminder.**
Apologies — occasionally a payment arrives without the invoice reference and takes longer to match. Send us the date and amount and we'll tie it up. *(Sam: this is the unreferenced-credit case in `runbooks/reconciliation.md` §4.)*
